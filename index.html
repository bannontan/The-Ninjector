The Ninjector

The foolproof Self-Injecting Solution that creates a seamless experience for caregivers in palliative care.

Problem Statement

Design a means to increase the confidence of caregivers when administering syringe-based medications

*Caregivers typically lack medical training

Features

After studying the practices of our target audience, caregivers, these features were incorporated into the solution to increase their confidence.

Drug Identification

Sensors identify the drug loaded and an OLED screen that reflects the name of the drug.

Friendly Packaging

Familiar and intuitive design that removes the needle from sight.

Last Injection Time Tracking

OLED screen reflects the time of last injection for the loaded drug.

Virtual Monitoring by Professionals

Device sends data for doctors to monitor injections in real-time.

Locking Mechanism

Locks the syringe in place, ensuring that the syringe does not move when the injection is being administered.

Chopping Mechanism

Ensures the optimum needle penetration depth and hides the needle from the users view.
